FOOD

Daily Meal

Our lovely "extra" pup has oatmeal for breakfast (lol) just put it in the small square dish and microwave for 8 seconds. She'll love it. Other than that, she gets 2⁄3 Cup of food daily. Will graze if she's hungry but will mainly eat in the evening.


Water

Usually drinks throughout the day. Will probably gulp a ton down after some good play.


Snacks

Has 3 different treats she can have once a day:

  • Cranberry Treat (red label)
  • Allergy Treat (gold label)

She can also have 1 to 2 pieces of apple, banana, or cucumber!
